Last updated: March 21, 2026

I operate personal automation services through SMS/MMS messaging. This policy describes what data is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected.

## What Data Is Collected:

- Phone numbers of known family members who have been directly added to a group text
- Message content received via SMS/MMS for the purpose of automated task and calendar management

## How Data Is Used:

Message content is processed by an automated system to create personal tasks and reminders, add events to a personal calendar, and send confirmation or follow-up messages to the group.

## Data Sharing:

Data is never shared with third parties, sold, or used for marketing purposes. Message content is processed solely for personal family coordination.

## Data Storage:

Message data is processed in real time and stored only as needed for task and calendar management. Data is retained on secure, access-controlled infrastructure.

## Consent and Opt-Out:

All recipients are known family members who have given direct verbal consent to participate. Any participant may opt out at any time by texting STOP to the messaging number.

## Contact:

For questions about this policy, visit jasonhutch.dev.
