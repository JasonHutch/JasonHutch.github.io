## Welcome

Hello! I'm **Jason Hutchinson**, a Lead Developer at Rightpoint and graduate student in Software Engineering at UT Arlington. I build secure, scalable backend systems and AI-powered tools on Azure and am always looking for new ways to bring intelligence into the software I design.

### What I Do

I specialize in architecting distributed systems and agentic AI applications. Day to day, that looks like:

* Building robust APIs in **C#/.NET** and **Python/FastAPI** across Azure cloud infrastructure
* Designing serverless tools on **Azure Functions** that power AI agents for document generation, BI automation, and enterprise integration
* Orchestrating LLM-driven workflows with **LangChain**, **Google ADK**, and protocols like **A2A** and **MCP**
* Collaborating with stakeholders to turn complex business problems into scalable, production-ready solutions
